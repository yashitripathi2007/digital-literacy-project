## **Stay Safe Online – Checklist for VIT Students**



1. Never click links in unsolicited emails/SMS — type the official URL yourself.

2\. Enable two-factor authentication on every account.

3\. Verify UPI requests by calling the official number — never share OTP.

4\. Use virtual UPI IDs and set transaction limits.

5\. Install official antivirus and keep phone OS updated.

6\. Do not share screenshots of OTPs or bank details.

7\. Report suspicious activity immediately to National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or dial 1930 (24×7).

8\. Use strong, unique passwords managed by a password manager.

9\. Think twice before sharing personal info on social media.

10\. Educate one friend every week about these tips.
