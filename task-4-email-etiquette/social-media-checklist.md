### **Do’s**



1. Use professional profile pictures and bios

2\. Enable two-factor authentication

3\. Think before posting — will this help or hurt my future?

4\. Engage positively and support classmates

5\. Regularly review privacy settings



### **Don’ts**



1. Share exam papers or confidential college info

2\. Post negative comments about faculty or batchmates

3\. Accept friend requests from strangers

4\. Use slang or abusive language in comments

5\. Overshare personal location or contact details
